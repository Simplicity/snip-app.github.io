Dashboards

Use Dashboards to arrange your notes neatly on a column-based layout. Drag-and-drop notes between columns and create as many columns and nested groups as you want.

Whiteboards

Use Whiteboards for a more visual arrangement of your notes. Connect notes with lines to indicate relationships. Freely zoom in and out for a better overview.
