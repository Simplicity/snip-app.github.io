Offline First

Your data is cached on your local disk by default. Being online is optional.

Privacy First

You can choose whether your data shall be synchronized. We support saving to the File System and our own Snip Cloud.

Different kinds of notes

Choose from different note types such as Rich Text, Code Snippets, Todo Lists and Sticky Notes

Tags are Folders.
Folders are Tags.

Use tags in the classic sense, e.g. lists. Use a slash, like private / plans, to organize your content in a folder-like structure.

Quick Access

Links and Code Snippets are accessible instantly with the Cmd+K shortcut.
Pressing Enter will copy the contents to your Clipboard.
